Expert Answers

How do I join the community or book a session?

Simply click the "Join The Community" button to connect with our front desk concierge via WhatsApp. We highly recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to secure your preferred sanctuary slot.

Do I need to bring anything with me?

Not at all. Our F-10 sanctuary provides everything required for your curated experience, including plush robes, comfortable slippers, and premium luxury toiletries. Just bring a desire to disconnect.

Are your therapists certified?

Absolutely. All of our holistic healers, spa therapists, and yoga instructors are highly certified professionals with extensive experience in premium wellness therapies.
